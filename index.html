Students can and have made a difference in the world we live in today. College Students Change the World believes that students' stories offer relatable exemplars who inspire others to become leaders of positive impactful change. This is the first phase in an online archive of primary and secondary source documents collected by Dr. Karen D. Boyd, colleagues, and University of Tennessee students.

Dr. K. Boyd collected and produced these primary sources and shared them here to support researchers’, scholars’, and educators’ efforts. Use the following APA citation if referencing or distributing content from this website, unless given a more specific preferred citation provided by the administrators of this website archive and collection of educational resources. This site also includes educational resources which integrate these historical findings and provides a theoretical framework for collecting your own stories.

Boyd, K. D. (2020). College Students Change the World Website – [Insert Title of the Document or Page – Format Description]. https://www.csctw.org/. (Date Accessed).
